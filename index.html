Our Departments & Services

Oil and Gas

Comprehensive engineering solutions for upstream and midstream oil and gas projects including natural gas gathering, processing, LNG engineering, transportation, and storage. We ensure safety, efficiency, and sustainability throughout project lifecycles.

Power Station

Design, construction, and maintenance of power generation facilities with a focus on reliability, efficiency, and compliance with environmental standards. Our team supports thermal, hydro, and renewable power projects.

Bulk Liquid Storage

Engineering and installation of bulk liquid storage tanks and facilities, ensuring secure, compliant, and efficient storage solutions for various industrial liquids and chemicals.

Cross Country Piping

Specialized in the design, installation, and maintenance of cross-country piping systems for oil, gas, and other fluids, emphasizing durability, safety, and environmental protection.

Tank Maintenance and Repair

Providing expert maintenance and repair services to extend the lifespan and ensure the integrity of storage tanks, minimizing downtime and operational risks.

Tank Inspection

Comprehensive inspection services using advanced techniques to assess tank condition, identify potential issues, and ensure compliance with industry standards and safety regulations.

Heavy Lifting

Professional heavy lifting and rigging services for industrial equipment and structures, utilizing modern cranes and equipment to safely handle complex lifting operations.
